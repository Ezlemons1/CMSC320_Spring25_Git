Q1: Why might we consider using Git?
Answer: Git is useful for tracking the updates and changes you make to your project files, as well as being streamlined
and efficient for collaborative work, crucial for project development.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.
Answer: Data science could definitely be applied to the artificial intelligence field. It could also
be applied to data analytics, finance, and language processing.

Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?
Answer: While merging applies the changes made on a specific branch onto the current branch, rebasing will take the
changes we make on another branch and replicate, or replay, those changes on the current branch.
